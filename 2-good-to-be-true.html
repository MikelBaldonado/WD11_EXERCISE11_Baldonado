2 Good 2 Be True is a Philippine drama romantic comedy television series broadcast by Kapamilya Channel. Directed by Mae Cruz-Alviar, it stars Kathryn Bernardo and Daniel Padilla.[1][2] The series premiered on the Kapamilya Channel's Primetime Bida evening block, A2Z, TV5, and worldwide via The Filipino Channel on May 16, 2022, replacing Viral Scandal. The drama marked the return of Bernardo and Padilla in primetime television five years after they last starred together in the 2017 series La Luna Sangre, under Star Creatives.
